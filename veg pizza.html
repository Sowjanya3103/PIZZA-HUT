Veg pizza

Bursting with rich aroma of herbs & spices and flavours, pizza is one of the most loved dishes of Italian cuisine.

Ingredients:

  • 2 readymade pizza base
  • 2 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chaat masala powder
  • 2 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon virgin olive oil
  • 3 tablespoon chopped black olives
  • 1/2 cup Pizza sauce
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 medium finely chopped tomato
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrot

Steps:

  1. Prepare the base sauce
  2. Saute the veggies
  3. Spread the sauce on the base
  4. Top with veggies
  5. Bake till golden-brown
  6. Serve hot with sauce
